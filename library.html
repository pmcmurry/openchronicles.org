Volume 1

Historia de Hierosolymitano itinere

Peter Tudebode (Petrus Tudebodus) · the First Crusade, 1096–1099

An eyewitness chronicle of the First Crusade by a priest who marched with the armies, newly translated from British Library, Harley MS 3904. Published in two editions: the source-faithful translation The Journey to Jerusalem and the narrative reader’s edition The Road to Jerusalem — each as free HTML and PDF.
