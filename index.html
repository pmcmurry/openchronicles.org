Two editions of each work

Each published work appears in both forms—clearly labeled.

Source-faithful

Source-faithful translation

Preserves substance, sequence, names, numbers, and the author’s rhetoric. Only the changes needed for readable modern English—no silent sanitizing or embellishment.

Reader’s edition

Reader’s edition

Continuous modern reading, based on the source-faithful text. No invented dialogue or events; medieval language is not softened for comfort.

What this is

Open Chronicles is an independent open-access project. English editions are prepared with AI assistance and human editing, then released free under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0.
